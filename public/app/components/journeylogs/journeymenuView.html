Journey to Prioria
Log 12: Harvest Report
Prioria is a great planet I’m sure is third in a fleet of seven deep-space ships engineered exclusively for the Galactic Zoo. Capable of quartering a Harvest/Science Crew of up to 88, the ship also has a standing operating crew of 112. The ship has no weapons but is equipped with a heavily fortified electromagnetic defensive shielding. The ship is fitted with complete living and recreational levels, an expansive observatory dome, research laboratories, hospital and extensive holding chambers.
